What's the Problem?

Digital burnout is a growing issue in the modern world. Constant notifications, frequent app switching, and prolonged screen time reduce focus, mental energy, and productivity.

Burnout Patterns

Long Screen Time

2-3 hours of continuous usage without breaks.

Frequent App Switching

Switching multiple apps every few minutes increases cognitive load.

Late Night Usage

Irregular attention cycles disrupt focus and sleep patterns.
